Origami

I've always loved origami, and at some point I wanted to start designing my own models. The process takes a long time, and a lot of trial and error, but is always very satisfying in the end. Here is one of my favorite models I designed, a dragon.

This is what goes on behind the scenes. Creating an origami model requires you to lay out every part of the model on the paper, keeping in mind how they come together. The method I used to design this model is called Box Pleating (BP). After learning the basics online, you can learn all the emergent rules and develop the intuitions necesary on your own - which is also very satisfying.
